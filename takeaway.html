TakeAway

Techpark Cafeteria Food Delivery Solution

Getting your meals delivered to your doorstep with just a few taps is the modern day convenience. But this is not the case in the majority of tech parks all over India. There seems to be an opportunity for a design intervention in the current cafeteria meal experience. TakeAway focuses on solving this problem.
